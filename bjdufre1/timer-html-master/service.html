TEDxSVSU is back for our second annual TEDx Conference!

How much do tickets cost?

General admission ticket prices are $25 for attendees. Seating is free for volunteers, speakers, exhibitors and sponsors.

How do I buy tickets?

Call (989) 964-4295. Tickets are available now, but seats are very limited. Only 100 tickets will be sold in total. Whether you pay by check or by credit card, please call to confirm your seat.

How do I pay by credit card or PayPal?

All you have to do is click on this link!

How do I pay by check?

Preferred form of payment is by check. Please make check payable to Saginaw Valley State University, referencing TEDxSVSU in the memo line. You can mail your $25 check to SVSU Sponsored Programs
